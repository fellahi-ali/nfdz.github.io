Me any day at work

Who am I?

I am a cheerful, reliable, team player and communicative person. Lover of the technological challenges, constantly improving. I love to learn from others and surround myself with people who give me worth.

I am a software engineer and developer with a very varied background currently specializing in mobile development and in particular Android. I have been lucky enough to study and work in many different fields.

There is nothing in this world I admire more than the ability to beat the security of the known and go beyond the comfort. I think business culture is very important and in the last few years many companies have developed new approaches in their way of working and I know I could contribute in many ways to them.

Professional Objectives

My goal is to develop my capacity as an engineer, contribute everything I can always to the company I belong to and learn every moment to grow personally and professionally.

I have many ideas all the time. However, an idea is worthless if I am not able to execute it correctly. So, I'd rather be able to run a bad idea than wasting my time thinking hundreds of mind-boggling ideas.

Throughout my career I've touched the entire Full-Stack, I like the idea of being able to create a complete product on my own. In this last stage of my career I specialize in Java technologies, especially in mobile development with Android.

I had to deal with management tasks, being responsible of small projects and staff, applying agile methodologies, such as scrum. Although for the time being, I have been mainly engaged in engineering tasks I do not discard in the future to learn and work more in the management part since I think it is vital to be part of both worlds.

My goal as a basketball player is to achieve to do beautiful dunks

02/2015 - 11/2016

Satellite ground control software developer In this work I was lucky to be able to work on the development of a new and very innovative product from scratch. I learned a lot with my workmates and team leader, whom I appreciate very much and I thank him for all the time he dedicated to me. Over time, more and more responsibilities were delegated to me, such as the control of the schedule, meeting of clients, documentation, user training, deliveries, management of people in my charge, specification of new requirements and features, choice of technologies and development lines.
